About

Discriminator is an experiment by Aldon Chen for Haptics and Optics, Fall of 2020. Using a generative adversarial network (GAN), I collected 78 procedurally genereted photo portraits of people that don't exist. In Discriminator I used this collection to make a series of animated portraits (800px by 800px true size, 300px by 300px on website) which attempt to study the biases in artificial intelligence and algorithms by finding key elements the GAN attributes to certain groups.
